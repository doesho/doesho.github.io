More Exceed in Early 2025?

News | December 24th, 2024

Something may be coming for Exceed in the first months of 2025, according to a comment made by Brad Talton.

In their update video for 2025, Level 99 Games looked back on their projects in 2024 and reveals a few projects coming in early 2025. However, Exceed is entirely absent from the video. Shortly after the video's publication, Brad made this comment in the Level 99 Games Exceed channel;

Brad: We haven't announced everything for 2025 just yet—only the first part of the year. There should be some news in the coming months.

Fans speculate that Guilty Gear Strive: The Board Game was not a financial success due to multiple factors, most infamously the miscolored card backs. However, a retail-scale Strive Season 2 is still a strong possibilty, as well as a Seventh Cross season reprint or rebalance that was hinted at last August. Finally, a previous survey suggested Bullet Exceed was under consideration.

JANUARY 2024

Introducing the Tabletop Simulator Card Counting Overlay

Community | January 24th, 2024

Breakfast Club member Inkie has uploaded a card counting overlay for Tirankin's Exceed Fighting System: Complete table.

This addon is loaded on top of the Exceed Fighting System: Complete, while you're at the table. It automatically displays what cards are possible for both players by remembering face-up cards shown in a player's area. The overlay is visible to players and spectators, so match commentary should be a lot easier now!

Check it out and subscribe to the workshop addon here!

With this addon and the recent browser client, Exceed netplay is entering a new age.

Exceed: Now For Your Web Browser

Community | January 12th, 2024

Breakfast Club member DT has begun development on a video game port of Exceed, playable in your browser!

Using the Godot engine, DT has captured the core gameplay and implemented all of season 7, plus a few characters from season 6 and 5, with more coming down the line. There's netplay with a lobby and matchmaking, as well as bot support, so you can always be playing when your boss or teacher isn't looking!

You can play the project here, hosted on itch.io.

If you'd like to give feedback or contribute to this project, you can find its dedicated channels in the Breakfast Club server, including github repos for both the client and server.

We'll see if the life cycle of this beautiful project ends in a DMCA, giving it the honorable death of all great fan projects. If not, we just might see this client come to replace Tabletop Simulator as the usual way to play Exceed online.

Guilty Gear -Strive- The Board Game 292% Funded!

News | September 12th, 2023

The Kickstarter campaign for Guilty Gear -Strive- The Board Game has completed at $439,003 raised. With an original goal of $150,000, that means it came $290,003 over the funding goal! That's 292%!

We can only hope this success gives them momentum as they negotiate for a bigger retail release, as well as rights to create the Strive DLC characters. Brad commented on this in the Countdown stream;

I think we'll have a lot more coming out for Guilty Gear."
Brad: "Nothing confirmed, I still have to clear the Arc Zaibatsu licensing hurdles, but I am excited to do more with Guilty Gear, at the very least making this available in retail size packaging."

AUGUST 2023

The TTS Module Is No Longer Called Balanced Breakfast

Community | August 28th, 2023

The day has come... Tirankin's tabletop simulator module, the addon that keeps Exceed glued together, has changed its funny name to something more normal.

Formerly known as XFS: Balanced Breakfast, the module is now known as Exceed Fighting System: Complete, with a new image featuring the logos of Exceed's season IPs.

The origin of "Balanced Breakfast" comes from Level 99 Games' BattleCON, their first fighting card game. The name BattleCON sounds like Bacon, and Exceed sounds like Eggs, thus bacon and eggs.
