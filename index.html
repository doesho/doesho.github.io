WHAT IS

?

Exceed is a crossover fighting game in card game form! It has the depth and interactivity of a fighting game with the variety and strategy of a card game, all in one Pick a character, represented by a 30 card deck, and fight your foe one-on-one - no arcade stick or deck building needed.

What makes Exceed special?

As a crossover game, Exceed features fighters from tons of franchises, like Shovel Knight, Street Fighter, Guilty Gear Strive and Dead Cells, with each fighter meticulously detailed to capture their nuance - and any fighter can fight any other, regardless of origin!

  • ...and more!

[Exceed is deep, has high skill expression, and each moment is filled with action. It is greatly enjoyable at all skill levels and gets better each time you play, even with the same characters.]

[cool specifics that sell the game] My favorite parts are the careful details that actually affect gameplay. Hadokens put themself back on top of your draw deck, so you can spam them in a projectile war. Hitting the opponent with a Sweep makes them discard a card, simulating knockdown by removing their options. Hit attack cards go to Gauge, which is spent to use your powerful Ultra attacks.

How can I play?

Are there communities for Exceed?

Yes! There are players of all skill levels online, with a thriving custom characters scene, online tournaments, matchmaking and tons of guides. The official Level 99 Games discord server has a few channels for Exceed. The Breakfast Club is the biggest dedicated fan server, run by community leaders, previous Exceed designers and developers of the Tabletop Simulator module.
