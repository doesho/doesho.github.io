WHAT IS EXCEED?

Exceed (AKA the Exceed Fighting System) is a crossover fighting game in card game form! It has the neutral, mixups and footsies of a fighting game with the nuance and economy of a card game, all in one. Pick a character, represented by a ~30 card deck, and fight your foe one-on-one.

What makes Exceed special?

As a crossover game, Exceed features fighters from 11+ franchises, including Shovel Knight, Street Fighter, Blazblue and Dead Cells, with each fighter meticulously detailed to capture their nuance - and any fighter can fight any other, regardless of origin!

Exceed also captures the nuance of fighting games, with added card game strategy. Footsies evolve naturally as fighters try to get into their preferred attacks range. Hit attacks go to Gauge, which is spent to use your powerful Ultra attacks. Each card in a figher's deck can be an attack, a boost or a resource, making every card valuable.

There's a plethora of fun details too! Ryu's Hadoken puts itself back on top of your deck, so you can use it again next turn. Hitting the opponent with the Sweep normal makes them discard a card, simulating okizeme by removing their options.

Some speculation believes this will be Season 7, but this is in contrast to the last official statement that Season 7 will be announced after Dead by Daylight finishes fulfillment, which will be far sooner.
