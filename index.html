Season 7 Update

May 30th, 2023

The most recent Level Up Live featured no mention of Bald Eagle (Season 7) or Exceed. Brad mentioned having the "prototype" in-hand for a project coming to Kickstarter in August. Some speculation believes this will be Season 7, but this is in contrast to the last official statement that Season 7 will be announced after Dead by Daylight finished fulfillment, which will be far sooner.
