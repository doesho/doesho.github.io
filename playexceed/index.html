WHAT IS EXCEED?

Exceed (AKA the Exceed Fighting System) is a crossover fighting game in card game form! It has the depth and interactivity of a fighting game with the variety and strategy of a card game, all in one. Pick a character, represented by a ~30 card deck, and fight your foe one-on-one, no arcade stick needed.

What makes Exceed special?

As a crossover game, Exceed features fighters from 11+ franchises, including Shovel Knight, Street Fighter, Blazblue and Dead Cells, with each fighter meticulously detailed to capture their nuance - and any fighter can fight any other, regardless of origin!

  • ...and more!

Exceed also captures the nuance of fighting games, with added card game strategy. Footsies evolve naturally as fighters try to get into their preferred attacks range. Hit attack cards go to Gauge, which is spent to use your powerful Ultra attacks. Each card in a figher's deck can be an attack, a boost or a resource, so every card has valuable uses.

There's a plethora of careful details too, capturing the feeling of a fighting game! Hadokens put themself back on top of your draw deck, so you can spam them in a projectile war. Hitting the opponent with a Sweep makes them discard a card, simulating okizeme by removing their options.

Are there communities for Exceed?

Yes! There are players of all skill levels online, with a thriving custom characters scene and online play. The official Level 99 Games discord server has a few channels for Exceed. The Breakfast Club is the biggest dedicated fan server, run by community leaders, previous Exceed designers and developers of the Tabletop Simulator module.
