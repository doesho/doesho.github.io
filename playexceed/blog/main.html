Brad: "We're going to announce news about Exceed in the near future"

News -- June 26, 2023

During the Q&A section of the June 26th Level Up Live, youtube user Jonathan Is Champ asked "What about season 7 of exceed??" Brad and Marco responded;

Marco: "Chat is asking 'What about Season 7 of Exceed?' Just you wait, that's the answer to that one! Just you wait!"
Brad: "We're going to announce news about Exceed in the near future. So wait up, you won't be disappointed."

The company's previous statement was that Season 7 would be announced following Dead By Daylight finishing fulfillment. In the May 30th Level Up Live[link?], Brad said he has a prototype in-hand for their "next project", which is debuting on Kickstarter in August. Some speculation believes this will be Season 7.

As far as IP speculation, the leading assumption is Guilty Gear -Strive-, which is developed by Arc System Works, the creators of BlazBlue and publishers of both BlazBlue and Under Night In-Birth. The project name for Season 7 is Bald Eagle, a possible reference to the bald eagle shown flying before every match of Guilty Gear -Strive-. It may also reference the game's themes of personal freedom and the prominence of the United States in the game's main story.

Besides Guilty Gear, further bald eagle related guesses have included G.I. Joe and My Hero Academia - the latter of which is owned by Jasco, who Level 99 Games have worked with in the past. Brad has expressed reluctance on working with them again after a transfer of ownership, however.[source?]
